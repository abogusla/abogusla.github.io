FemtoBrew logo design

Femto Brew is a new cider and mead brewery located in Ottawa, Canada. The brewery was founded by husband and wife team, Kevin and Sarah. They specialize in small batch ciders and meads, made from locally-sourced fruits and honey. Femto Brew offers a variety of flavors, from classic apple ciders to more adventurous flavors like wild cherry and raspberry. They also offer a range of meads, including traditional mead, flavored meads and even sparkling meads. Femto Brew is dedicated to producing the highest quality craft cider and mead, and their products are available in select stores and restaurants in the Ottawa area.
