Recipe Title: Sweet Potato Tahini Bowl

Ingredients:
- 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped into 1/2 inch cubes
- 2 tablespoons of extra-virgin olive oil
- 1/4 teaspoon of salt
- 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
- 2 tablespoons of tahini
- Juice of 1 lemon
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 2 tablespoons of chopped fresh parsley

Instructions:
1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
2. Place the chopped sweet potatoes onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
3. Drizzle the olive oil over the sweet potatoes and season with salt and pepper.
4. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes, flipping once halfway through.
5. In a small bowl, whisk together the tahini, lemon juice, garlic, and parsley.
6. Remove the sweet potatoes from the oven and transfer to a bowl.
7. Drizzle the tahini mixture over the sweet potatoes and stir to combine.
8. Serve warm and enjoy!
