Ham and Turkey Egg Sandwich recipe



Recipe Title: Ham and Turkey Egg Sandwich

Ingredients:
- 3 slices of bread
- 2 slices of ham
- 2 slices of turkey
- 2 eggs
- Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:
1. Heat a skillet on medium heat.
2. Crack the eggs into a small bowl and whisk together.
3. Add salt and pepper to the eggs, to taste.
4. Pour the eggs into the heated skillet and scramble until cooked through.
5. Toast the slices of bread.
6. Layer the ham and turkey onto one slice of toast.
7. Place the scrambled eggs on top of the ham and turkey.
8. Place the remaining slice of toast on top of the eggs.
9. Cut the sandwich in half and enjoy!
